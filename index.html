Our Mission

10MINS WITH FAMOLUWA is more than a podcast. It’s a movement to shape the lives of Christians by spending just 10 minutes with God’s Word every week. This podcast brings clarity, comfort, and conviction to listeners through deep truths presented in a simple way.

Meet Your Host - Famoluwa

Famoluwa is a passionate Christian podcaster, youth leader, and Bible teacher. With a heart for truth and transformation, each episode is packed with divine wisdom, practical application, and a call to action.
